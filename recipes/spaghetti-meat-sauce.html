Spaghetti and Meat Sauce

Description

A classic home cooking favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. box of Spaghetti
  • 16 oz. jar Ragu Traditional sauce
  • 1 lb. ground beef

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef. Drain.
  2. Combine sauce and beef, bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low.
  3. Boil a pot of water.
  4. Add pasta and boil 7-11 minutes until tender. Drain.
  5. Combine pasta and sauce. Serve.
