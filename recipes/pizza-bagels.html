Pizza Bagels

Description

Nummy comfort food at its best.

Ingredients

  • 12 frozen Lenders Bagels
  • 8 oz. Jar pizza sauce
  • 1 lb. Mozzarella cheese, shredded

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Heat bagels in microwave for 30 seconds on high to soften.
  3. Slice and separate bagels into round halves.
  4. Coat each half with light amount of sauce.
  5. Cover generously with mozzarella and arrange on a cookie sheet.
  6. Bake until cheese is melted.
