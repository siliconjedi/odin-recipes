Chicken Soup

Description

Kerry's famous chicken soup is delicious and hearty, a family favorite demanded by sick O'Shea's everywhere.

Primarily flavored by the interplay of carrots and parsnip, with a kick of dill, this noodle free soup is the perfect complement to an crisp autumn day.

Ingredients

  • 4 32 oz. Boxes College Inn Chicken Broth
  • 10-12 chicken drumsticks
  • 2 lbs. carrots, peeled and chunked
  • 2 lbs. parsnips, peeled and chunked
  • 1 purple top turnip, peeled and chunked
  • 1 stalk celery
  • 1 yellow onion
  • plain parsley to taste
  • dill to taste
  • 8 oz. can yellow corn
